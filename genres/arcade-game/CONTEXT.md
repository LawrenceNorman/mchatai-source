# Arcade Game

Arcade games are characterized by simple controls, immediate feedback, and escalating difficulty, often featuring short, repeatable sessions aimed at achieving high scores. These games typically involve navigating a playfield filled with obstacles and enemies, with a focus on accessibility and addictive gameplay loops.

## When this genre is the right seed

Classifier keywords: arcade game, centipede, pixel graphics, high score, retro gaming, shooter, wave counter, bonus points, mushrooms, 4-directional movement

## Seeded wisdom rules

1. Player movement is typically restricted to a specific area of the playfield, such as the bottom fifth of the screen.
2. Enemies often enter from the top of the screen and move in predictable patterns, which players must learn to anticipate.
3. Shooting mechanics should provide immediate visual and auditory feedback to enhance player engagement.
4. The game should include bonus elements, such as a spider that grants extra points when shot, to encourage risk-taking.
5. A clear and visible score system is essential to motivate players to improve and beat their previous high scores.
