# Arcade Shooter

Arcade shooters are fast-paced games where players control a ship to destroy incoming threats, often featuring simple mechanics, retro graphics, and an emphasis on high scores and reflex-based gameplay.

## When this genre is the right seed

Classifier keywords: asteroids, arcade shooter, retro graphics, high score, UFO, wrap-around screen, vector graphics, fast-paced action, precision shooting

## Seeded wisdom rules

1. The player ship must be able to rotate and thrust in any direction for precise control.
2. Asteroids should split into smaller pieces when hit, increasing the challenge as the game progresses.
3. The game should feature a wrap-around screen where objects reappear on the opposite side.
4. Periodic appearances of a UFO add an extra layer of challenge and scoring opportunities.
5. Scoring should be based on the size of asteroids destroyed, with larger asteroids yielding more points.
