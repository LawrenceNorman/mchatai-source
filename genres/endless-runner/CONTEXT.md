# Endless Runner

An endless runner is a fast-paced arcade game where players control a character that automatically moves forward, requiring them to navigate obstacles and collect items to achieve high scores. The game continues until the player makes a mistake, with difficulty escalating over time.

## When this genre is the right seed

Classifier keywords: endless runner, auto-run, high score, obstacle navigation, pixel art, arcade, fast-paced, scoreboard

## Seeded wisdom rules

1. Endless runners should have simple controls that allow players to focus on timing and reflexes.
2. The game must feature escalating difficulty to maintain player engagement.
3. Visual and audio feedback should be immediate to reinforce player actions.
4. A high score system is essential to encourage replayability and competition.
5. The game should include obstacles that require quick decision-making and precise timing.
