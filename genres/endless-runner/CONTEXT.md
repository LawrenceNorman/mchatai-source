# Endless Runner

Endless runner games feature continuous, fast-paced gameplay where players navigate obstacles to achieve high scores. They are characterized by simple controls, increasing difficulty, and procedural generation, making them ideal for mobile platforms.

## When this genre is the right seed

Classifier keywords: endless runner, flappy bird, subway surfers, temple run, obstacle navigation, high score, procedural generation, fast-paced, mobile game

## Seeded wisdom rules

1. The player must tap or swipe to navigate through obstacles, maintaining a balance between challenge and accessibility.
2. Gravity or constant forward motion should be a core mechanic, requiring players to time their actions precisely.
3. Obstacles must be procedurally generated to ensure each playthrough feels unique and challenging.
4. The game should end immediately upon collision with an obstacle, reinforcing the high-stakes nature of the genre.
5. Visual variety, such as a day/night cycle, can enhance player engagement without altering core gameplay mechanics.
