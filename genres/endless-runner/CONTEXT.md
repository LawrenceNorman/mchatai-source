# Endless Runner

Endless runners are fast-paced, auto-scrolling games where players aim to survive as long as possible by avoiding obstacles and collecting items, typically using simple touch controls. They are characterized by vibrant visuals and progressively challenging gameplay, making them ideal for mobile platforms.

## When this genre is the right seed

Classifier keywords: endless runner, Jetpack Joyride, Subway Surfers, Temple Run, auto-scrolling, obstacle avoidance, coin collection, fast-paced

## Seeded wisdom rules

1. The game world MUST auto-scroll horizontally, with the player character positioned on the left third of the screen.
2. Tapping and holding anywhere on the screen SHOULD activate the jetpack or equivalent mechanic, causing the character to ascend.
3. Obstacles such as stationary lasers, missiles, and rotating zappers MUST be avoided, with game over occurring upon first collision.
4. Coins or other collectibles SHOULD be arranged in arcs and lines to encourage strategic pathing.
5. The game MUST track distance traveled and coins collected, displaying these at the top of the screen.
