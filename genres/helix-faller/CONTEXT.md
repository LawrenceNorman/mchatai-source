# Helix Faller

Helix Faller games involve rotating a vertical tower to guide a falling ball through gaps in platforms, avoiding obstacles and progressing through levels. The genre is characterized by simple controls, addictive mechanics, and a focus on visual and audio feedback.

## When this genre is the right seed

Classifier keywords: helix fall, tower rotation, falling ball, gap navigation, level progression, ball skins, obstacle avoidance, visual feedback

## Seeded wisdom rules

1. The player must be able to rotate the tower smoothly to align gaps with the falling ball.
2. Each level should increase in difficulty by adding more obstacles and fewer gaps.
3. Visual effects like flashes or particle effects should be used to enhance feedback when a level is cleared.
4. Unlockable ball skins provide a sense of progression and customization for players.
5. Sound effects for ball bounces, level clears, and game over events should be included to enhance the audio experience.
