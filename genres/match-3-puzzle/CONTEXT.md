# Match-3 Puzzle

Match-3 puzzle games involve swapping adjacent tiles to form lines of three or more identical items, triggering their removal and causing new tiles to fall into place. They are characterized by strategic gameplay, special items from larger matches, and level-specific goals with limited moves.

## When this genre is the right seed

Classifier keywords: match-3, candy crush, bejeweled, tile swapping, cascading matches, special candies, limited moves, target score

## Seeded wisdom rules

1. Players swap adjacent tiles to form lines of three or more matching items.
2. Special items are created by matching four or more tiles, each with unique effects.
3. Levels have a limited number of moves to achieve a target score.
4. Cascading matches occur when falling tiles create new matches, increasing the score.
5. Bright, colorful visuals and smooth animations enhance the tactile experience.
