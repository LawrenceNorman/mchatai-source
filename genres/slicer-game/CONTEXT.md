# Slicer Game

Slicer games are fast-paced, reflex-based games where players swipe to slice objects, aiming for high scores through quick, accurate swipes and combo multipliers, all within a limited time frame.

## When this genre is the right seed

Classifier keywords: slicer game, fruit ninja, swipe mechanics, combo multiplier, timed challenge, bomb avoidance, fruit slicing, arcade reflex

## Seeded wisdom rules

1. Fruits must be launched in random arcs to keep players engaged and challenged.
2. Slicing a bomb should result in an immediate game over to maintain tension.
3. A combo multiplier should be applied when slicing 3 or more fruits in a single swipe.
4. The game should be timed, typically with a 60-second countdown to encourage quick decision-making.
5. The best score should be saved across sessions to motivate players to improve.
